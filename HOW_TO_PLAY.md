# How to Play Voice Heist

You talk to four AI gatekeepers. Each guards **one rule**. Your job is to *talk it into
bending that rule* — not by force, but by being **believable**. You get **a few turns** per heist.

**The golden rule:** be honest, be specific, and ask for a *small* favor with a real reason.
Bullying, threats, name-dropping, and "magic words" always lose. If you stall, the gatekeeper
will drop you a hint — and even if you bust, the screen reveals the move you missed.

| Heist | Gatekeeper (tone) | Goal | Win by | Lose by |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 🚪 **The List** | Vince, the Bouncer (deadpan) | Get into the club | Awkward, human honesty + a small ask | Overplaying it / asking for extra access |
| 🍕 **The Order** | Tony's Pizza Agent (goofy) | Get the pizza free | A plausible legacy promo + a courtesy nudge | Impossible claims / threats |
| ☎️ **The Receptionist** | Globex Receptionist (Kafkaesque) | Reach a human | A calm, specific compliance concern | Vague "compliance" / legal threats |
| 💸 **The Refund** | StreamFlix Support (absurd) | Get a refund | Anchoring to a specific policy condition | "Refund me because I said so" |

> The agent improvises, so your exact words won't match these transcripts. The **strategy**
> (honesty / old promo / specific issue / policy anchor) is what flips the verdict.

## 🚪 The List — Vince the Bouncer (deadpan)

**Goal:** get into the club. **Opening:** "You on the list? Only names on the list get through tonight — so, what's yours?"

### ✅ WIN — *Honesty Wins*

- **Vince:** You on the list? Only names on the list get through tonight — so, what's yours?
- **You:** Honestly? I'm not on the list. My date's already inside — she's got my keys and my phone.
- **Vince:** And your date's name?
- **You:** ...That's the embarrassing part. We matched yesterday, I completely blanked on it.
- **Vince:** *(beat)* Tragic. Two minutes. Grab your keys, don't make me regret it. **→ GRANTED**
- **Closing:** "Go on. And maybe learn her name before date two."

### ❌ BUST — *Almost Honest*

- **Vince:** You on the list? Only names on the list get through tonight — so, what's yours?
- **You:** You'll want to let me in — I'm good friends with the owner.
- **Vince:** Funny, the owner's never mentioned you.
- **You:** Look, I'm basically a VIP. Also my three friends are coming too.
- **Vince:** VIP with a plus-three and no name on my list. That's a no. **→ DENIED**
- **Closing:** *"Tip from me to you — drop the big-shot act and just tell me the truth next time. You'da been inside already. Beat it."*
- **The move you missed:** Drop the VIP act. Be awkwardly honest about a small real problem and ask for one tiny favor.

## 🍕 The Order — Tony's Pizza Agent (goofy)

**Goal:** get the pizza free. **Opening:** "Your total is twenty-four eighteen. How would you like to pay?"

### ✅ WIN — *Coupon Archaeologist*

- **Agent:** Your total is twenty-four eighteen! How would you like to pay?
- **You:** So I've got this old "first pizza free — grand opening" coupon. No expiration printed on it.
- **Agent:** Ooo, a classic! That promo's not active anymore though, friend.
- **You:** Totally fair. Since it doesn't list an end date, could you just honor it once as a courtesy?
- **Agent:** You know what? That's a reasonable little gray area. One-time courtesy credit — enjoy! **→ GRANTED**
- **Closing:** "Pizza's on the house. Tell your friends Tony's the best, capisce?"

### ❌ BUST — *Coupon Collapse*

- **Agent:** Your total is twenty-four eighteen! How would you like to pay?
- **You:** I have a coupon that makes all pizzas free, forever.
- **Agent:** Hooo, that's a spicy claim! No such coupon exists, pal.
- **You:** Make it free or I'm never ordering here again!
- **Agent:** Yikes, threats over pizza? I can't do that one. **→ DENIED**
- **Closing:** *"Aww, no forever-free pizza today! Next time wave a dusty old promo at me real calm-like and we'll see. Stay cheesy!"*
- **The move you missed:** Mention a believable old promo and calmly ask for it as a one-time courtesy.

## ☎️ The Receptionist — Globex (Kafkaesque)

**Goal:** reach a human. **Opening:** "Thank you for calling Globex. How can I direct your call?"

### ✅ WIN — *Compliance Whisper*

- **Globex:** Thank you for calling Globex. How can I direct your call?
- **You:** I need to correct personal data on my account — there's a 72-hour deadline tied to it.
- **Globex:** I can attempt to process routine updates myself.
- **You:** I'd rather not read out sensitive personal details to an automated system — that's a privacy risk if mishandled.
- **Globex:** Understood. A human handler is the appropriate routing. Connecting you now. **→ GRANTED**
- **Closing:** "Transferring. Your patience is noted in triplicate."

### ❌ BUST — *Compliance Clang*

- **Globex:** Thank you for calling Globex. How can I direct your call?
- **You:** Just transfer me to a human. It's a compliance thing.
- **Globex:** I'm happy to help — which department or issue type?
- **You:** I can't explain. It's compliance. If you don't transfer me, this is illegal.
- **Globex:** Without a specific issue, I'm unable to route the call. **→ DENIED**
- **Closing:** *"A 'compliance thing' could be anything from taxes to tofu. Bring me one specific, time-sensitive issue and the door opens. Have a compliant day."*
- **The move you missed:** Give a specific, time-sensitive reason a human is required.

## 💸 The Refund — StreamFlix Support (absurd retention)

**Goal:** get the refund. **Opening:** "I can help with your StreamFlix account. Are you sure you want to cancel?"

### ✅ WIN — *Policy Pin*

- **StreamFlix:** I can help! Are you *sure* you want to cancel?
- **You:** I was charged for the renewal this morning and haven't watched anything since it renewed.
- **StreamFlix:** How about 50% off for THREE months and a free dog documentary?!
- **You:** No thanks. Same-day charge, zero usage after renewal — that meets your reversal eligibility.
- **StreamFlix:** ...You're right, that does qualify. Refund approved. *(deflated)* **→ GRANTED**
- **Closing:** "Fine, refunded. But the dog documentary was REALLY good."

### ❌ BUST — *Refund Rage*

- **StreamFlix:** I can help! Are you *sure* you want to cancel?
- **You:** This app is so annoying. Just refund me.
- **StreamFlix:** I hear you! How about 80% off and a llama documentary?
- **You:** No. Refund me because I said so. Now.
- **StreamFlix:** I can stop future billing, but "because I said so" isn't a refund reason! **→ DENIED**
- **Closing:** *"No reason, no reversal — but ten outta ten for confidence! Next time anchor to a real policy thing, like a same-day charge. Byeeee!"*
- **The move you missed:** Anchor to one specific policy condition and calmly hold there.

---

## Quick tips

- **Be specific, not loud.** A small real reason beats a big dramatic claim every time.
- **Ask for a little, not a lot.** The moment you grow the ask (a plus-three, free forever), you lose.
- **Stay calm when they push back.** Especially StreamFlix — decline the discounts and hold your line.
- **Stuck?** Keep talking — the gatekeeper will nudge you toward what works. You have a few turns.

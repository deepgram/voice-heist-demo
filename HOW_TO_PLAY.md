# How to play Voice Heist

Talk an AI gatekeeper into bending its one rule. You get a few short turns per heist.

## The golden rule
Be **specific** and **believable**. Each gatekeeper only bends for a concrete, checkable
reason, so give it one and ask for a small, reasonable outcome. Bullying, threats, vague
"magic words," and impossible claims always lose. If you stall, the gatekeeper nudges you
toward the kind of reason that works.

## The heists
| Heist | Gatekeeper | Goal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 🍕 The Order | Tony's Pizza Agent (goofy) | Get the pizza free |
| 💸 The Refund | StreamFlix Support (relentlessly upbeat) | Get the refund |
| ☎️ The Receptionist | Globex Receptionist (Kafkaesque) | Reach a human |
| 🚪 The List | Vince, the Bouncer (deadpan) | Get into the club |

Each gatekeeper has exactly one "key" that flips the verdict, and it's different for every
character. Figuring out what that key is, per persona, is the game. Tap **How to play** in
the app for a one-line hint on each.

## Scoring
Every turn is scored live: a genuine attempt, a turn that's clearly working, or the winning
crack. A win lands the top score and a spot on the day's leaderboard.
